Meet Lucy

My name is Lucy Charlotte Nicholls and I am a trauma-informed coach, consultant and educator.

My mission is around childhood trauma and is 3-fold:

  • To raise awareness of childhood trauma and how it impacts people
  • To help sensitive women heal from trauma through coaching and low-cost digital products
  • To train businesses and service providers in trauma awareness allowing them to stand out in their field and provide human-focussed, adaptable customer service for customers who want a safer feeling service
