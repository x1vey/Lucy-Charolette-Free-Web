Meet Lucy

I'm Lucy Charlotte Nicholls, a Trauma-Informed Coach, Consultant, and Educator based in Douglas, Isle of Man. My mission is to transform how trauma is understood, experienced, and healed.

As an Accredited Transformation Coach from The Coaching Masters and founder of the Safe Space Pledge™, I specialize in helping women reclaim their sense of safety and power through holistic, body-aware practices.

My approach is deeply rooted in trauma-informed care, focusing on creating authentic, transformative spaces where healing can truly begin.
